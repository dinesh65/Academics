About Me

Assistant Professor of Mathematics & Basic Sciences at NIIT University, Rajasthan, India.

I specialize in mathematical modeling of biological populations using tools from dynamical systems, bifurcation theory, spectral graph theory, and optimal control. I aim to bridge rigorous mathematical theory with applications in biology, ecology, and epidemiology.

Research Interests

  • Dynamical systems and bifurcation theory
  • Graph partitioning and spectral graph theory
  • Stability of complex biological and social networks
  • Optimal control theory for population dynamics
  • Numerical methods for data science
